Our Story

We are passionate travelers dedicated to sharing Cambodia's hidden gems with the world. Founded in 2020, KnowMore started as a small blog and has grown into a comprehensive travel resource.

Our team of local experts and travel enthusiasts work together to bring you authentic experiences, insider tips, and carefully curated destinations across the Kingdom of Cambodia.
